Key Features:
-------------
1. Freight Request Management
   - Create, update, publish, and close freight requests
   - Detailed cargo, route, and schedule information
   - Status tracking through lifecycle

2. Bidding System
   - Carriers submit competitive bids
   - Detailed pricing breakdowns
   - Bid acceptance/rejection workflow
   - Automatic contract generation

3. AI Matching Engine
   - Intelligent carrier scoring (0-100)
   - Multi-factor analysis:
     * Route expertise (25%)
     * Price competitiveness (20%)
     * Reliability (25%)
     * Fleet suitability (20%)
     * Schedule compatibility (10%)
   - Personalized recommendations

4. Contract Management
   - Digital contract creation
   - Electronic signatures
   - Document management
   - Multi-party signing workflow

5. Carrier Profiles
   - Fleet information
   - Certifications
   - Route expertise
   - Performance ratings
   - Statistics tracking

6. Search & Discovery
   - Route search
   - Carrier search with filters
   - Market statistics
   - Pricing trends

7. Alerts & Notifications
   - Price alerts
   - Route availability alerts
   - Carrier availability
   - Configurable frequencies

Database Collections:
---------------------
- freightrequests  # Freight requests from shippers
- bids             # Carrier bids
- contracts        # Awarded contracts
- carriers         # Carrier profiles
- alerts           # User alerts
